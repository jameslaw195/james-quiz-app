Virginia Woolf Quiz

"Mrs. Dalloway said she'd buy the flowers herself." So opens Woolf's 1925 stream of consciousness novel. What is its title?

Mrs. Dalloway

The name of Woolf's lady friend, lover, and the inspiration for her fantastical, gender-bending 1929 biography-cum-novel Orlando.

Vita Sackville-West
